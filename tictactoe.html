RULES

  1. -Tic-Tac-Toe is a game that is played on a grid of 3x3.
  2. -Player 1 is 'O' & Player 2 is 'X'. Use your mouse to select a tile on the gameboard.
  3. - The first player to get 3 turns in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner.
  4. - When all 9 squares are full, the game is over. If no player has 3 in a row, the game ends in a tie.
  5. - Click SAVE to save your score on the Leaderboard.
